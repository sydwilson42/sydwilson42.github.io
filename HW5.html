Steps to Playing Basketball in College!

  1. Make your high school team!
    • Practice!!!
    • Work on important skills like ball-handling or shooting.
    • Talk to the coach to see what they are looking for.
    • Go to try-outs and show out!
  2. Join a good AAU summer team!
    • Important things to look for in a team
      1. Your team needs to fit your style of play
      2. You need to have a coach you can trust and bond with (because you may need their help with recruiting)
      3. You want to join a team that is competitive and fits your skill level so you will benefit from this experience
    • Use these tournaments and practices to improve your skills during the summer
    • These are great exposure tools for college coaches!
  3. Respond to all your recruiting contacts!
    • College coaches may email, text, call, or send a you a letter when trying to contact you. You must watch for these things so you can respond in a timely manner!
    • Some may contact you through your high school or AAU coaches, so make sure you ask them as well.
    • Lastly, make sure all responses are formal and timely, so they know you are interested and professional.
  4. Stay on top of applications, offers, etc!
    1. Always apply ASAP after a coach asks you to do so. They will notice if you delay on things like this!
    2. Next you must send transcripts as soon as you can because they can take a few days to actually deliver
      • If you delay on this, it will delay your acceptance into school!
    3. If you really like an offer, do not wait too long to accept it because it can be taken away.
    4. Lastly, if you know you are not going to choose a school who has offered you, just be honest. They may need to offer it to someone else.
